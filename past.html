Wondering Where I Came From?

My Roots

Family of Origin

I grew up with my mom, dad, two younger sisters and our family dog.

Location

I grew up in Virginia, pretty close to D.C. When I was in my late 20s, I moved out of my parents' house - for real this time! - to Denver, CO, and spent 6 or 7 years out there playing in the mountains.

Former Hobbies and Interests

Fantasy and Horror

As a high schooler, I read so many fantasy books, including Harry Potter and Lord of the Rings, and I started a scary movie club with my friends.

Travel

When I lived near D.C., I used to travel internationally as much as I could. I lived in Qatar for almost a year, and New Zealand for a couple of months.

Outdoor Activities

In Colorado, I spent a lot of my free time hiking and snowboarding -- my favorite mountain out there is A Basin. (Don't have a favorite here yet but I'm open to suggestions!)

Indoor Activities

I also practiced various types of yoga at different studios around town, and taught myself how to cook at 30 years old. And by taught myself, I mean that I invited my friends who could cook over to my place and watched what they did and participated as much as they would let me.

Previous Career

Teaching

I attended the University of Virginia for undergrad, where I studied English literature, and then for grad school, where I studied Instructional Technology in the Education school. I started out my career journey as an international teacher, and quickly realized that I didn't want to live that far away from my family all the time. I also taught middle school technology for a year in Denver Public Schools, and quickly realized that I didn't want to be a K-12 teacher in the U.S.

Software Engineering

So I pivoted and started dabbling in computer stuff, first as a data analyst and then as a web developer via General Assembly bootcamp. I worked as a software engineer for companies ranging from Fortune 500 clients to series A startups. It was a blast!
